My Teaching Notes!

University of Iceland

Here I will slowly add notes while teaching that I hope are of some use to the students. This is in hopes the material is seen in multiple ways and more importantly, is played with to encourage creative thought around physics instead of rigid procedures already exhausted by the course and books.
